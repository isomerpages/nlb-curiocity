Welcome to our interactive timelines that showcase the development and milestones of Singapore's most architecturally significant buildings. Using a vast collection of library and archival materials such as photographs, maps, building plans, and architectural publications, we guide you through the evolution of these iconic structures. 

Join us on a journey through time as we explore the stories behind Singapore's architectural marvels.
